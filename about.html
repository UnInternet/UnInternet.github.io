about uninternet


UnInternet was born in 2022 with the ideas of Gabriel Fernandez, he says that the internet has a finite time, and if we do not worry about it, it might end. the idea of UnInternet is to replicate old web aesthetics in the modern web, websites such as NeoCities are already doing this, but not in such a manner
